ABOUT ME

Modern day web developer with modern day skills

Welcome to my webpage, thank you for visiting. My name is Tristan and I'm a professional web developer. I work for Bath Fitter in Dayton, OH as the web applications developer. My interest in computers started from a young age. My father taught me everything he knew and could from the minute I could understand it. He helped me understand what the computer does, how it does it and how I can make it do what I want. He spent his life working as an IT Administrator and while that is interesting to me, my heart eventually fell for software.

While web developemnt as a whole was not my crusade, I practiced my C++ skills through various ways. Mostly from reverse engineering video games and reconstructing them as emulators and sandboxes (shells of their existing selves). This was always a passion of mine. Along the way, I did learn some basic CSS and HTML but never anything major until I found myself out of work and looking for a new job. I was hired on at Bath Fitter (yeah, the bathtub people). There I learned masses about web applications and the development thereof. I learned the newest and best technologies including, but not limited to, Angular, React, Vue.js, Firebase and GraphQL. These things made my life easier and as I I got used to the different style of work, I developed a fondness for web development.

As I discovered my skills for web development were, unbeknown to me at the time, better than I thought they could be. I began looking into freelance work to develop websites. I have taken on some projects but I am in search of more work and a wider audience to reach in order to get my work out there and attract more business. I have an array of skills that could be an asset to any person, company or enterprise. No matter the scale. I have done flat static webpages, dynamic webpages, animated webpages, simple and complex web applications and CRMs/Dashboards. These projects vary in difficulty, but no matter the task, I'm up for the challenge.
